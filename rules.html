Game Rules

Welcome to Art-Match!

Objective:

Match pairs of identical images hidden behind cards to earn points and advance through levels.

Game Setup:

  • Each level features a collection of cards.
  • Level 1: 9 cards (4 pairs + 1 spare card).
  • As you progress, more cards and pairs will be added.

How to Play:

  1. Find Pairs: Flip cards to reveal images. Match pairs by remembering their locations.
  2. Earn Points: Matched pairs earn points. The faster you match, the more points you get.
  3. Advance Levels: Complete each level by matching all pairs to unlock new challenges.

Lives:

  • Start with 3 lives.
  • Lose a life for each incorrect match.
  • Be strategic! Conserve lives to reach higher levels.

Scoring:

100 points awarded for each matched pair.

Winning:

Reach the highest level and achieve the highest score to become the Art-Match champion! (In this demo version there are only 5 levels)
